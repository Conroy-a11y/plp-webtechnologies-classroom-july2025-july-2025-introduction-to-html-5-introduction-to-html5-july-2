Learning about SEO

What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the practice of optimizing web pages to rank higher in search engine results, thereby increasing the visibility of a website.

Why is SEO important?

SEO is important because it helps drive organic traffic to a website, improves user experience, and builds credibility and trust with users. Higher search rankings can lead to more clicks and conversions.

Key SEO Techniques

  • Keyword Research: Identifying the right keywords to target.
  • On-Page SEO: Optimizing content, meta tags, and URLs.
  • Off-Page SEO: Building backlinks and improving domain authority.
  • Technical SEO: Ensuring the website is crawlable and indexable by search engines.
